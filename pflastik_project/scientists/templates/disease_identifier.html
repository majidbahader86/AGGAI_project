Latest Plant Diseases

Late Blight

A devastating disease affecting tomatoes and potatoes, caused by the oomycete Phytophthora infestans. Symptoms include dark lesions on leaves and stems, and can lead to rapid plant death.

Powdery Mildew

A common fungal disease affecting various plants, characterized by a white powdery coating on leaves. It can reduce plant vigor and yield if left untreated.

Fire Blight

A bacterial disease affecting apple and pear trees. Infected branches appear as if scorched by fire. It can cause significant damage to orchards if not managed properly.

Leaf Spot

A group of diseases caused by various fungi and bacteria, characterized by spots on leaves. While often cosmetic, severe cases can lead to defoliation and plant stress.
